french onion soup

We have been trying French onion in resturants for years and my family and friends agree none can compare to my recipe for taste and simplicity of preparation

1 h 5 m

4 servings

585 cals

Ingredients

  • - 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • - 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • - 4 cups sliced onions
  • - 4(10.5 ounce) cans beef broth
  • - 2 tablespoons dry sherry (optional)
  • - 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • - salt and papper to taste
  • - 4 slices French bread
  • - 4 slices provoione cheese
  • - 2 slices swiss cheese, diced
  • - 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
Prep
15 m
Cook
50 m
Ready in
1 h 5 m

Directions

1) Melt butter with oilve oil in 8 quart stock poy on medium heat. Add onions and continualty stir until tender and translucent. Do not brown the onlons.

2) Add beef broth, sherry and thyme. Season with salt and papper, and simmer for 30 minutes

3) Heat the oven broiler

4) Ladle soup into oven safe serving bowls and place one slice of bread on top of each (bread may be broken into pieces if you prefer). Layer each slice of bread qith a slice of provoione. 1/2 slice diced Swiss and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese. Place bowls on cookie sheet and broil in the preheated oven
